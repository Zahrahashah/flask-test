Eligibility Criteria

Applicants must fulfill the following conditions:

  • Be between 18 and 50 years of age
  • Possess a valid Disability Certificate from the Department of Social Welfare
  • Be free from contagious diseases and substance abuse
  • Have no criminal record or association with political organizations

Admission Policy Guidelines

Nasheman provides vocational training and rehabilitation services to persons with disabilities under the policies of the Social Welfare Department, Government of Punjab.

Applications are considered based on:

  • Compliance with eligibility requirements
  • Submission of complete, verified documents
  • Availability of hostel accommodation (where applicable)

Additional Information:

  • Hostel accommodation is available for male trainees only
  • Female trainees may avail the pick-and-drop transport service
  • The institution reserves the right to accept or reject any application as per government regulations
