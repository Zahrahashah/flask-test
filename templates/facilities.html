Facilities & Support

Free Accommodation & Meals

Providing a nurturing environment with comfortable living and nutritious meals.

  • Free, well-maintained hostel accommodation
  • Healthy, nutritious meals provided daily
  • Safe, supportive environment promoting independence

Stipend Support

Financial assistance to encourage consistent attendance.

  • Residential Trainees: Rs. 1,500/month
  • Day Scholars: Rs. 1,000/month
  • Helping reduce financial stress

Hostel Accommodation

Comfortable living spaces for our trainees.

Category Capacity
Physically Disabled20
Mentally Challenged10
Visually Impaired (Blind)10
Hearing & Speech Impaired10
Total Capacity50 Residents

Hostel accommodation is for male trainees only. Female trainees are provided pick & drop transport facilities.

Comprehensive Support

Holistic care for every trainee's well-being.

  • Free Medical Care: Government hospital treatment available when needed.
  • Religious & Moral Education: Developing character through ethical and religious teachings.
  • Employment Assistance: Guidance for securing jobs under the 3% disability quota.

Extracurricular Activities

Engaging programs for holistic development.

  • Sports & Physical Activities: Inclusive sports like wheelchair cricket.
  • Literary Programs: Poetry and storytelling sessions.
  • Cultural Events: Festivals and group celebrations.
  • Recreational Outings: Nasheman Sports Club outings.

Community-Driven Support

Guided by a dedicated advisory committee.

  • Guide improvements
  • Ensure transparent operations
  • Manage donations for student welfare
